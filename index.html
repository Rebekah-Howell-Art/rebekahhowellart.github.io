Meet the artist

Rebekah Howell is an artist who brings depth and emotion to life through charcoal drawings. Inspired by her faith and personal reflections, her work explores storytelling and themes of love, identity and connection.

Her mission is to make art that feels personal, purposeful and holds a genuine connection with the viewer.
